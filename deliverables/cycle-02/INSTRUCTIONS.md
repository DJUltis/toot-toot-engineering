# INSTRUCTIONS

Title: Tula the Tulip and the Magic Garden Map
Tagline: Press, fold, and bloom a paper flower.

## Before you start
- You need the two plates and a square sheet of origami paper.
- Center the paper in the plates before pressing.

## Steps
1) Press the plates. The garden map appears.
2) Fold along the big diagonal lines to make an X.
3) Fold along the center lines to make a plus sign.
4) Bring the bottom corner up to form the tulip base.
5) Fold the side corners in to make petals.
6) Open the top to bloom the tulip.

## Illustration notes
- Use big step numbers and one picture per step.
- Show the map lines as light dashed lines.
- Show the active fold as a bold line with an arrow.
