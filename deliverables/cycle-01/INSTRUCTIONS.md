# INSTRUCTIONS

Title: Kiko the Crane and the Magic Map
Tagline: Press, peek, and fold to help Kiko fly.

## Before you start
- You need the two plates and a square sheet of origami paper.
- Make sure the paper is centered in the plates before you press.

## Steps
1) Slide the paper into the plates. Press gently. The map appears.
2) Fold along the big diagonal lines to make an X. Smooth the folds.
3) Fold along the center lines to make a plus sign. Smooth again.
4) Make the "kite" shape using the map lines. Flip and repeat.
5) Pull up the neck and tail. Then fold down the wings.
6) Open the wings. Kiko can fly.

## Illustration notes
- Use big step numbers and one picture per step.
- Show the map lines as light dashed lines.
- Show the active fold as a bold line with an arrow.
